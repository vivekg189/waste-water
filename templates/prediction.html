How to read this

The model fits a straight line through your sum of liters per calendar day. If usage is flat, the forecast stays flat; if it climbs or falls, the line extends that slope. Sudden one-day spikes (events, holidays) will tilt the forecast — pair with the dashboard’s spike alerts.

Improve accuracy

  • Log at least 3 days; 7+ is better
  • Keep missing days empty rather than estimating
  • After major changes (new residents), wait for a fresh week
Forecast is indicative — not a replacement for tank alarms or municipal metering
